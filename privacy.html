Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-24

AI Budget Bits provides a private utility application that helps our own TikTok creator account publish original videos via TikTok’s Content Posting API.

Data We Process

  • OAuth tokens: Stored locally by the operator to upload and publish to their own TikTok account.
  • No end‑user personal data: We do not collect personal data of other TikTok users.
  • Logs: Local technical logs for debugging (success/failure of posts).

Use of Data

Tokens are used strictly to post to the authenticated owner’s account. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

Retention & Deletion

Tokens are kept locally and can be deleted at any time by the operator. Access can also be revoked in TikTok settings.

Children's Privacy

Not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.
